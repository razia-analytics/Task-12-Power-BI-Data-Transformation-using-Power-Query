HR Analytics Dataset
1. The HR Analytics dataset was imported into Power BI Desktop.
2. Data cleaning and preparation were done using Power Query Editor.
3. Unnecessary columns such as Employee Count, Over18, and Standard Hours were removed.
4. Column names were renamed for better readability.
5. Correct data types were assigned to all columns (numeric and text).
6. Missing values were handled by removing or replacing null values where required.
7. Conditional columns were created:
o Age Group from the Age column
o Salary Band from the Monthly Income column
8. All transformations were reviewed in the Applied Steps pane.
9. Changes were applied using Close & Apply, and the data was loaded into the model.
10. Finally, the dataset was cleaned, structured, and made ready for HR attrition analysis and visualization.
